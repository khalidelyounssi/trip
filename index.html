Architecture & Kasbahs

From the intricate Zellige mosaics of imperial cities to the earthen fortresses (Kasbahs) of the south. We take you to Telouet Kasbah and Ait Ben Haddou to witness the ingenuity of Berber architecture.

The Salt Route & Idrissides

Founded by Abdou Idrissi, descendant of Morocco's first dynasty. We retrace the ancient Salt Route where white gold was traded for spices. From Zagora to Timbuktu, every grain of sand tells a story.
